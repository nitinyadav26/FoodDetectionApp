FoodSense - AI-Powered Nutrition Tracker

SUBTITLE: Scan Food, Track Nutrition, Get Healthier

DESCRIPTION:
FoodSense is your intelligent nutrition companion that makes healthy eating effortless. Simply point your camera at any meal and let AI instantly identify the food and provide detailed nutritional information.

KEY FEATURES:

- AI Food Scanner: Take a photo of any meal and get instant nutritional analysis powered by Google Gemini AI. Identifies Indian and international dishes with calorie, protein, carb, and fat breakdowns.

- Offline Food Detection: Built-in YOLOv8 machine learning model detects 95+ Indian food items without an internet connection. Works anywhere, anytime.

- Barcode Scanner: Scan packaged food barcodes to instantly look up nutrition facts from the Open Food Facts database.

- Smart Nutrition Tracking: Log meals with automatic portion scaling. Track daily calories, protein, carbs, and fats against personalized goals calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation.

- AI Health Coach: Get personalized nutrition advice, recipe suggestions, and motivation from your AI-powered health coach that understands your eating patterns and goals.

- Health Integration: Syncs with Apple Health (iOS) and Health Connect (Android) for steps, active calories, sleep, and hydration data.

- Smart Scale Pairing: Connect your Bluetooth smart scale for automatic weight-based portion calculations.

- Daily Insights: View nutrition trends, streaks, and earn badges for consistent healthy eating habits.

- Beautiful Dashboard: At-a-glance view of your daily nutrition, health metrics, and food log with date navigation.

- Privacy First: Your data stays on your device. Optional cloud sync with Firebase. No ads, no selling data.
