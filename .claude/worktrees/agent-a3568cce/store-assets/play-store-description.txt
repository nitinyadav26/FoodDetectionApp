SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars):
AI-powered food scanner & nutrition tracker. Scan meals, track calories, get fit.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
FoodSense uses artificial intelligence to make nutrition tracking effortless. Point your camera at any meal for instant nutritional analysis, or scan barcodes on packaged foods.

FEATURES:
- AI Food Scanner: Instant nutrition analysis powered by Google Gemini AI
- Offline Detection: 95+ Indian foods recognized without internet using on-device ML
- Barcode Scanner: Look up packaged food nutrition via Open Food Facts
- Smart Tracking: Log meals with automatic portion scaling and personalized calorie goals
- AI Health Coach: Personalized advice based on your eating history and health goals
- Health Connect: Sync steps, calories, sleep, and hydration data
- Bluetooth Scale: Connect smart scales for portion-based logging
- Streaks & Badges: Stay motivated with daily logging streaks
- Light & Dark Mode: Beautiful Material You dynamic theming on Android 12+
- Multilingual: English and Hindi support
- Privacy First: Data stays on device, optional cloud sync
