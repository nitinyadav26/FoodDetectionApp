FoodSense - AI-Powered Nutrition Tracker

SUBTITLE: Scan Food, Track Nutrition, Compete with Friends

DESCRIPTION:
FoodSense is your intelligent nutrition companion that makes healthy eating effortless and social. Point your camera at any meal and let AI instantly identify the food and provide detailed nutritional information. Log meals by voice, chat with an AI nutritionist, compete in challenges with friends, and earn badges as you build healthier habits.

KEY FEATURES:

AI Food Scanner
Take a photo of any meal and get instant nutritional analysis powered by Google Gemini AI. Identifies Indian and international dishes with calorie, protein, carb, and fat breakdowns. Includes food quality scoring (0-100), portion estimation, and before/after plate analysis.

Voice Food Logging
Say what you ate and FoodSense logs it automatically. Hands-free meal tracking powered by on-device speech recognition.

Offline Food Detection
Built-in YOLOv8 machine learning model detects 95+ Indian food items without an internet connection. Works anywhere, anytime.

Barcode & Label Scanner
Scan packaged food barcodes to instantly look up nutrition facts from the Open Food Facts database. Nutrition label OCR reads and logs packaged food info directly from photos.

Smart Nutrition Tracking
Log meals with automatic portion scaling. Track daily calories, protein, carbs, and fats against personalized goals calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation.

AI Nutritionist Chat
Get personalized nutrition advice, recipe suggestions, and motivation from a persistent AI health coach that remembers your eating patterns, goals, and conversation history.

7-Day Meal Plans
AI-generated weekly meal plans tailored to your calorie targets, dietary preferences, and nutritional goals.

Weekly Insights & Predictions
Visual charts showing your nutrition trends, weight predictions, and actionable recommendations to improve your diet.

Daily Nutrition Quiz
Test and grow your nutrition knowledge with AI-generated daily quizzes. Earn XP for correct answers.

Social & Friends
Add friends, view their activity, and share your nutrition wins. Generate a QR profile card for easy connection.

Activity Feed
See what your friends are logging, celebrate their milestones with emoji reactions, and stay inspired by the community.

Challenges
Create or join nutrition challenges with friends. Track progress on shared goals like calorie targets, protein streaks, or logging consistency.

Leaderboards & Leagues
Compete on friends, global, weekly, and monthly leaderboards. Climb through league tiers from Bronze to Diamond based on your XP.

Gamification & XP System
Earn XP for logging food (+10), maintaining streaks (+5/day), unlocking badges (+25), winning challenges (+50), and acing quizzes (+15). Progress through 50 levels and 7 titles from Newbie to Legend.

50+ Badges
Collect badges across 6 categories: Streak, Logging, Nutrition, Social, Challenges, and Special. Track your collection and showcase achievements on your profile.

Health Integration
Syncs with Apple Health for steps, active calories, sleep, and hydration data.

Smart Scale Pairing
Connect your Bluetooth smart scale for automatic weight-based portion calculations.

Home Screen Widget
Glanceable calorie and macro tracking right on your home screen.

Privacy First
Your data stays on your device. Optional cloud sync with Firebase. No ads, no selling data.
